Stephen Lance McDaniel
Dad, Husband, Coder

A little about me: I identify as a father to three amazing little kiddos ages 4, 8, and 12, a loving husband of 17 years, a coding enthusiast and lifelong learner, a Jiu Jitsu martial artist (current rank = brown belt), an Army Veteran and a war veteran, and a coffee lover.

I started my coding journey about 2 years ago when I decided that I wanted to learn more about how the robotic equipment I was operating in NDT was both developed and actually operated. My need to understand what was under the hood was driven by the gap I found between my knowledge with how to work with these machines in Non-destructive testing and their capabilities in the real world. Through a combination of personal connections, youtube, and google exploration I continued to seek more knowledge about coding and robotics. I recognized early that the future of NDT inspection and maintenance is in the robotic industry and that manipulating machines and large amounts of data is vital to the growth and safety of our world.
